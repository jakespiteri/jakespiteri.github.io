About

I am a PhD Student under the COMPASS CDT in the Institute for Statistical Science, University of Bristol. Prior to my PhD I completed an integrated master’s degree in Mathematics with Statistics at the University of Bristol.

My research interests include:

  • Bayesian methods,

  • High-dimensional statistics,

  • Computational statistics,

  • Graph theory.

You can contact me at jake.spiteri@bristol.ac.uk.
